Get ready...

For the adventure of a lifetime with Travel Rafting, the leading rafting company in Peru! From the roaring rapids of the Cañete River to the crystal-clear waters of the Apurímac, we offer experiences that will challenge your spirit and ignite your passion for adventure.

History

Travel Rafting, located in Lima, Peru, was founded by Arturo Mendez, an adventurous enthusiast who transformed his love for rivers and mountains into an internationally recognized rafting agency.

With a focus on safety, education, and environmental preservation, Arturo and his team offer thrilling rafting excursions on rivers such as the Cañete and Apurímac, connecting visitors with Peru's rich culture and providing unforgettable experiences in the wild!
